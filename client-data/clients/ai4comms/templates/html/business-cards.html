PRINT SPECIFICATION

Dimensions: 85 × 55 mm (EU standard). Bleed 3 mm on all sides.

Stock: 400 gsm uncoated (Munken Pure or equivalent). Soft tactile finish reinforces the "quiet authority" brand voice.

Ink: CMYK process. Match Phosphor #44E88A as a spot color (~PMS 2275 C) where budget allows.

Foil option: Phosphor accents (cursor, edge bar, URL) can be spot-UV or foil-stamped for premium variants.
